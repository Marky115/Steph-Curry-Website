About Steph Curry

Steph Curry or known as Wardell Stephen Curry is 4x Championship winning Point Guard that currently plays for the Golden State Warriors team based in San Francisco. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest point guard of all time and as the greatest 3 point shooter of all time with a record of over 3000 3 pointers made.
